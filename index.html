Mesha Naidoo-Edwardson

Computer Science Student, Software Developer, & Athlete

About Me:

Hi, my name is Mesha. I am a third year student at the University of Waterloo pursuing an Honours Bachelors of Computer Science.

In my free time, I enjoy ultimate frisbee, bouldering, and adventuring with friends. I have been playing ultimate since 2016 when my brother inspired me to try out for the competitive junior team in Ottawa. Since, I've travelled across North America and Europe to play in both fun and competitive tournaments. I recently picked up bouldering during summer 2022 and have been going consistently weekly due to how much fun it is. I love the social and athletic aspects. As I mentioned adventuring with friends, both of these activities and many more in my life have meant so much to me due to the people around.

As a developer, I have spent the majority of my time on the front-end, starting as a test automation engineer in 2020 and since working my way to being a front-end developer during summers of 2021 and 2022. Across these positions, I became comfortable using React, AngularJS, Typescript, Cypress, Jest. Starting in fall of 2022, I'm beginning to focus on expanding my back-end skills. I am currently working part-time alongside my studies using Node.js and Express.js. I am excited to venture into new subsets of software development and to become an individual who is versatile across many stacks.

Thank you for taking the time to learn about me, and please reach out if you're interested in connecting.
