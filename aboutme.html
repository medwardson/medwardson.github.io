Who am I?

Hello! As you already know, my name is Mesha. I am a second-year mathematics student at the University of Waterloo, pursuing an Honors Bachelors of Mathematics in Statistics, with a minor in Entrepreneurship. Furthermore, I am a member of the University of Waterloo Ultimate Frisbee Club, which just finished fourth at the University National Championships.

Sports are a large part of my life. On top of Ultimate Frisbee, I am also a participant in Spikeball and Volleyball Intramurals, and before University played competitive volleyball for 5 years. During the winter, I love to go downhill skiing. I spent a large part of my childhood at Mont-Tremblant, QC, where we would ski and snowshoe every weekend.
